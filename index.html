Hi, I'm Eliot.

I make videos and streams. Useful information when I can manage it, occasional rambling when I can't, and the odd story along the way. I'm still small enough that the platforms I post on don't pay anything yet, so this little page is a tip jar. If something I've made was useful, or kept you company for a while, drop something in.

Where it goes

For now, the money goes back into the work. Better gear, more time, fewer distractions, the boring overheads that let any of this keep happening.

The longer plan is something for the natural world. Conservation, animals, the environment. The exact shape isn't decided yet. There are a lot of directions and I'm still working through them. What I know is I'm aiming for it.
